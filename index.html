Cuelogic Technologies is a global company providing technology and engineering services to companies worldwide. Cuelogic offers offshore product development and technology services in niche areas like Web Applications, Mobile Applications, Cloud Computing and Advanced Testing Solutions for Enterprise and Consumer Software. Cuelogic also helps companies boost their in-house team’s capacity and capabilities by providing engineers and designers for temporary and long term assignments. The company has been trusted partner for product companies and world-class startups for many years now.

We have our offices in New York, California and our centre of excellence is based out of Pune, India. We are focussed on the following technologies: Python, Django, Golang, Ruby on Rails, PHP, NodeJS, JavaScript, AngularJS, Backbone.js, React.js, HTML5, D3, Scala, Java, .Net, Big-Data, DevOps, Mobile (iOS, Android, Blackberry, Windows, PhoneGap), MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB. Mission: At Cuelogic, we strive with passion for all things that make up product development. Committed to the goal of building world class software products, we believe in exceeding client expectations and solving challenges innovatively. A close, collaborative approach with clients and partners ensures exceptional quality products get built speedily with low risk. Primarily, our work entails making a difference in as many lives as we can, ensuring a better and happier world using technology.
